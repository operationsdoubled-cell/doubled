🌾 Our Story

A family tradition,
one loaf at a time.

The Golden Loaf was born from a simple idea: that bread should taste like bread. Not the packaged, shelf-stable kind — but the kind that fills a kitchen with warmth and comes out of the oven with a perfect crackling crust.

Founded by the Hargrove family in 2009, we've grown from a single market stall to a beloved Bristol institution — but our values have never changed. Local. Honest. Made with love.

  • Only locally-sourced, stoneground flour from Wessex Mills
  • No preservatives, additives, or shortcuts — ever
  • Slow-fermented sourdough for real flavour and gut health
  • Seasonal specials that celebrate what's fresh right now
